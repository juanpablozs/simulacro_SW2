# Soluciones al problema 2

## Apartado 1

db.listingsAndReviews.find({}, {name:1, number_of_reviews:1, _id:0}).sort({number_of_reviews:-1}).limit(2)


## Apartado 2

db.listingsAndReviews.find({}, {name:1, amenities:1}).sort({'amenities.length':-1}).limit(2)



## Apartado 3
